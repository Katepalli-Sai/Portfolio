Projects

Local Multi-Vendor Website (LMVW)

The Local Multi-Vendor Website (LMVW) is an innovative web-based platform designed to bridge the gap between local vendors and their communities. By providing a centralized online marketplace, this website empowers local businesses such as grocery stores, stationery shops, electrical goods vendors, fruit sellers, flower sellers, and more to enhance their visibility and accessibility to customers. Customers benefit from the convenience of browsing and purchasing from multiple local vendors online, accessing diverse products, fast delivery, and supporting their local economy. Local Multi-Vendor Website aims to strengthen local economies, foster community connections, and provide a seamless shopping experience that promotes small businesses and enhances customer satisfaction.

Task Project

The Task Project is a comprehensive task management solution designed to enhance productivity and organization. It offers intuitive features for creating, prioritizing, and managing tasks, enabling users to stay on top of their responsibilities with ease. Users can add detailed task information, including titles, descriptions, deadlines, and priorities, ensuring a clear understanding of each task's importance. The ability to hide tasks allows users to focus on their current objectives without unnecessary distractions, while still retaining access to all essential information.
